At the aquarium

What do you know about sea creatures?

Level 2 Reading

There are so many other incredible sea creatures to discover at the City Aquarium, including octopuses, jellyfish, seahorses and starfish. Which is your favourite?

Shark

Sharks have lived in our oceans for over 400 million years. Some sharks are big, some are very small and some are very scary! Don't miss the sharks' feeding time at 3 p.m. every day in our shark zone.

Penguins

Most penguins are from the southern hemisphere, and some of them live in one of the coldest places on Earth: Antarctica. Watch our playful penguins dive and swim in the icy waters of our penguin zone!

Turtles

Turtles have been on planet Earth for over 200 million years. But today some species of sea turtle are endangered, because of hunters, fishing nets or plastic in the ocean. Come and see these beautiful creatures in our turtle zone.

Coral isn't a plant or a rock - it's a type of animal! Coral reefs are home to many species of tropical fish. Sharks, lobsters and turtles live here too. Discover this wonderful world in our coral reef zone!
